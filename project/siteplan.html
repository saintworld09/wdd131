School Project Website Plan

Site Name

Saintworld Academy

This name represents a central hub for our school's community, providing essential information and resources for students, parents, and faculty. The name was selected to convey the site's role as a primary source of information and a focal point for the school community.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the Saintworld Academy website is to provide a comprehensive and accessible platform for our school community. The site will offer detailed information on academic programs, extracurricular activities, faculty profiles, campus facilities, and upcoming events. It aims to support students, parents, and faculty by offering valuable resources and updates.

Scenarios

  • How can I find information about the school's extracurricular activities?
  • Where can I access the contact information for the faculty members?

Color Scheme

The color scheme for the Saintworld Academy website will be:

  • Primary Color: #2c3e50 (used for headings and navigation background)
  • Secondary Color: #ecf0f1 (used for background and content sections)
  • Accent Color: #e74c3c (used for links and buttons)

Typography

The typography for the Saintworld Academy website will include:

  • Heading Font: 'Arial', sans-serif (used for headings and navigation)
  • Body Font: 'Verdana', sans-serif (used for body text and content sections)
